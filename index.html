KSU FinTech Hackathon Champion 2025

Won against high school & college teams using Percolation Theory for stablecoin liquidity

Our team built Liquid, a payment intelligence concept that applies Percolation Theory from physics to model liquidity in stablecoin transaction routes. By treating exchanges and corridors as a network with occupation probabilities, we estimated success likelihood and identified optimal routing to minimize failed transfers.
